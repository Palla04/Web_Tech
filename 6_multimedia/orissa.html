Orissa

Orissa is a state on the eastern coast of India, known for its ancient temples, rich tribal culture, and scenic beaches. The Jagannath Temple in Puri, Konark Sun Temple, and the annual Rath Yatra festival attract millions of devotees. Odisha is also home to the Chilika Lake, a paradise for bird watchers, and the stunning waterfalls of Simlipal National Park.
